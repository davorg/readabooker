Book: Treacle Walker

Alan Garner 2022
Buy on Amazon

"Treacle Walker" by Alan Garner is a captivating exploration of time, reality, and folklore through the eyes of Joe Coppock, a young boy recovering from an eye ailment. Joe's life takes an intriguing turn when he encounters Treacle Walker, a wandering rag-and-bone man who trades seemingly mundane items imbued with mysterious powers. As Joe delves into these cryptic objects, he becomes increasingly entwined with the rhythms of his surroundings, transcending ordinary boundaries and touching the core of faerie-like mythology. Garner weaves a poetic narrative that blurs the lines between dreams and reality, infusing the tale with a deep sense of wonder. Rich in lyrical prose and vivid imagery, the novel is a spellbinding journey that invites readers to ponder the nature of perception, identity, and the interconnectedness of all things. Through Joe's unique adventures, Garner crafts a timeless story that resonates with both the fantastical and the universal, offering a thought-provoking reflection on the magic embedded in everyday life.
