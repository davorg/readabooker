Book: To Rise Again at a Decent Hour

Joshua Ferris 2014
Buy on Amazon

"To Rise Again at a Decent Hour" by Joshua Ferris is a darkly comic exploration of identity, faith, and the modern malaise associated with the digital age. The story follows Paul O'Rourke, a successful but deeply disenchanted dentist in Manhattan, whose life is thrown into disarray when someone begins impersonating him online. The mysterious occurrence sends Paul on a quest for meaning in a world where personal connections are strained and people cling to digital personas more than ever. This existential journey is rich with Ferris's sharp wit and insightful observations on the nature of belief and belonging. As Paul grapples with his past and his relationships, including those with his ex-girlfriend and his enigmatic office manager, he is forced to confront profound questions about who he is and what he stands for. Ferris deftly balances humor and introspection to create a poignant narrative that challenges the characters, and readers alike, to consider the deeper truths behind their everyday lives.
