Beginning with the arrival of a peculiar letter, the novel spirals into a multi-layered tale that intertwines the lives of its diverse characters, each marked by their own struggles and aspirations. The story is set against the backdrop of social upheaval and change, blending elements of mystery, political commentary, and dark humor.
