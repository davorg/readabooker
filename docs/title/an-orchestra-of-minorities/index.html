Book: An Orchestra of Minorities

Chigozie Obioma 2019
Buy on Amazon

"An Orchestra of Minorities" by Chigozie Obioma is a profound tale of love, destiny, and the tumultuous journey of a man seeking redemption. Set in Nigeria, the novel intricately explores the life of Chinonso, a humble poultry farmer whose world is irrevocably altered when he prevents a woman, Ndali, from committing suicide. This fateful encounter ignites a passionate love affair, but their relationship faces harsh opposition due to social class disparities.

Driven by the desire to prove his worth and secure a future with Ndali, Chinonso makes a fateful decision to pursue education overseas, leading him down a path fraught with unforeseen challenges and betrayal. Narrated by his chi, or guardian spirit, the novel weaves Igbo cosmology with a contemporary narrative to explore themes of sacrifice, destiny, and the struggles of the human soul.
