Book: Orbital

Samantha Harvey 2024
Buy on Amazon

"Orbital" by Samantha Harvey weaves a rich tapestry of interconnected stories set against the backdrop of a near-future space station, offering a profound exploration of human connection and the fragility of existence. The novel captures the lives of diverse characters, each grappling with their personal dilemmas as they orbit Earth in a quest for scientific discovery and personal redemption. Harvey deftly examines themes of isolation and camaraderie, as the crew members confront their inner demons and seek companionship in the vast, unending darkness of space. As tensions mount and secrets unravel, the characters must navigate not only the perils of space but also the complexities of their own emotions. With poetic prose and keen psychological insight, "Orbital" transcends the boundaries of a traditional sci-fi narrative, delivering a compelling meditation on identity, memory, and the bonds that tether us to one another even when light years apart. Samantha Harvey's storytelling melds atmospheric tension with philosophical depth, rendering a narrative that is both a gripping adventure and a thoughtful reflection on what it means to be human.
