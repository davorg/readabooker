About ReadABooker

ReadABooker is a website dedicated to the Booker Prize for Fiction. It allows you to browse Booker Prize winners and shortlisted books by year, author, and title.

The aim of the site is to make it easy to explore the history of the Booker Prize and discover books you may not have read yet.

This is an independent website and is not affiliated with the Booker Prize Foundation. All information is provided for informational purposes.

If you would like to get in touch about the site, please use the contact page.
