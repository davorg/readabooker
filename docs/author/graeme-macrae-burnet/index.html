Graeme Macrae Burnet is a celebrated contemporary author hailing from Kilmarnock, Scotland. With a keen eye for detail and a flair for storytelling, he has carved a niche for himself in the genre of literary crime fiction. Burnet pursued his education in English Literature at the University of Glasgow, where he developed a profound appreciation for narrative structure and character development. His works often blur the lines between fact and fiction, a hallmark of his unique narrative style.

His works have been translated into multiple languages, affirming his international appeal. Beyond writing, Burnet engages in discussions and lectures about the craft of writing, contributing to the literary community with both his insights and his creative works.
