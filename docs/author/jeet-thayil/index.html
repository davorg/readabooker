Jeet Thayil is an acclaimed Indian author, poet, and musician, born on October 13, 1959, in Kerala, India. Known for his deeply evocative prose and poetic style, Thayil's literary works often draw from his varied personal experiences and the vibrant settings of Indian cities. He grew up in a literary environment, as his father was a well-known journalist. Thayil's education spanned across several countries, including India, Hong Kong, and the United States, which enriched his diverse cultural perspective.

Prior to this novel, Thayil established himself as a prominent poet with several published collections that received critical acclaim. Besides his literary career, Thayil is also a talented musician, known for his experimental works with the band Sridhar/Thayil. His contributions to literature and the arts have earned him numerous accolades, and he continues to be a significant voice in contemporary Indian literature.
